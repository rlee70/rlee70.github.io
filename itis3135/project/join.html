To become an official member of the club, every student/player must both:

  • Pay dues at the start of each semester ($40 for returning players/$60 for new players)
  • Be registered “approved-active” on DSE to participate.

If you complete both of these, then you're an official UNCC Men's Club Soccer player! Once you do, we'll invite you to our GroupMe group chat
