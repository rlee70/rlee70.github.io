UNC Charlotte Men's Club Soccer is the largest sports club at UNC Charlotte and competes against other local/in-state college club soccer teams in both the fall and spring semesters. We highly encourage competitive players to come and tryout at the start of each semester, but we do NOT make cuts. Anyone is free to join the club! Visit our other pages for more information about us like game times, our roster, and how to join.

Follow us on Instagram (@unccmensclubsoccer) to stay up to date with important information!
