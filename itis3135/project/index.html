UNC Charlotte Men's Club Soccer is the largest sports club at UNC Charlotte and competes against other local/in-state college club soccer teams in both the fall and spring semesters. We highly encourage competitive players to come and tryout at the start of each semester, but we do NOT make cuts. Anyone is free to join the club!

Follow us on Instagram @unccmensclubsoccer to stay up to date with important information!
